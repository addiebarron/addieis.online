Welcome to my Twitter archive

In 2024, I deleted my Twitter account after 11 years, but not before downloading the full archive of my decade of posting. I think of this page as a memorial for the experience of being on the website and the words I published over those years. It's melancholic, but God is Change and that website had long overstayed its welcome.

On this page you can search my tweets or browse by date or popularity. Threads are kept together if you click through any individual tweet. This archive does not include replies, so you'll only see "standalone" tweets and threads.

This site was made with Darius Kazemi's Twitter archiving tool.
